Requirements

  • Be a high school senior living in New Jersey
  • Have battled or is battling pediatric cancer or a hematological disorder

Deadline: May 8th, 2023 at 11:59 pm ET

Details

  • Scholarship Payout:
  • The $48,000 scholarship will be distributed between the 3 recipients and paid in $4,000 increments each year directly to the University Bursar office.
  • Voting and Scoring:
  • Top 10 applications that garner the most "likes" will be reviewed and voted on by Childhood Cancer Society's Adventure Ted Scholars committee.
  • Adventure Ted Scholar Winners:
  • 3 Recipients will be announced at an awards ceremony on May 21, 2023 at 1 PM ET, hosted by American Dream (Court A, Level 1).
  • Will also receive an internship with the Childhood Cancer Society.
  • Scholar must produce 1 Adventure Ted Experience each year to remain eligible for subsequent tuition payments.

How to Apply

  1. Complete the Adventurer Application (link in bio of @adventureted.official or click link below)
  2. Create an Instagram post responding to the prompts in our post
  3. Tag @adventureted.official and @childhoodcancersociety in your post
  4. Stay tuned for updates and announcements about finalists
