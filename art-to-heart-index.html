Art to Heart Campaign

We understand that children with cancer face many challenges, both physical and emotional. That's why we are excited to announce the "Art to Heart Campaign". This contest is designed to provide an outlet for children to express themselves creatively, find hope in their struggles, and connect with others who understand their journey.

Our aim is to create an uplifting and encouraging environment for children with cancer to showcase their artistic talents and inspire others. Through this contest, we hope to encourage children to find strength, courage, and hope in their lives, despite the challenges they face.

Winners of this contest will be able to bring their families on an all-expenses-paid VIP Disney/Universal/Seaworld Vacation with 5-star lodging for a week at Give Kids The World Village.

Find out how to apply below!

Rules and Guidelines

  1. Must have pediatric cancer or have had pediatric cancer.
  2. Must not have already had a wish grant for a Give Kids The World Vacation or Disney vacation (this includes Disney cruises).

  4. Wish Grant Child must live in the USA.
